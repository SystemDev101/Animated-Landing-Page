Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: How long does it take to receive my refund?

Once we receive and inspect your returned item(s), refunds are processed within 3–5 business days. The timing for the refund to reflect in your account depends on your bank/payment provider (typically 5–10 business days).

Q2: What if my item arrives damaged or defective?

Contact us immediately at pandaworldclothin@gmail.com with photos of the damaged item and your order number. We'll either send a replacement or issue a full refund—no return needed!

Q3: Can I return an item without contacting you first?

No. You must email or call us to initiate the return process. Unauthorized returns (sent without approval) will not be refunded.

Q4: Do you offer exchanges?

We currently don't offer direct exchanges. To swap an item, return the original purchase for a refund and place a new order on our website.

Q5: What counts as "original condition"?

Items must be unworn, unwashed, and free of stains, odors, or damage, with all tags and packaging intact.

Q6: Can I return sale or discounted items?

Only full-priced items are eligible for returns. Final sale items (e.g., clearance) cannot be returned.

Q7: What if I miss the 7-day return window?

Unfortunately, we strictly enforce the 7-day policy. Late requests will not qualify for a refund or exchange.

Q8: How do I track my return?

Use the tracking number provided on your prepaid shipping label. Contact us if you don't receive confirmation within 5 business days of shipping.

Q9: What proof of purchase do I need?

Provide your order number (e.g., #PW1234) or the invoice emailed to you after purchase.

Q10: Does the 7-day window start from the purchase date or delivery date?

It starts from the purchase date (the day you placed your order).
