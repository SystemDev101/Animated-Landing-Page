Panda World Clothing Shipping Policy

Fast, reliable delivery to get your fashion favorites to you as quickly as possible!

1. Processing Time 1-2 Days

+
  • All orders are processed within 1-2 business days (excluding weekends and holidays).
  • Orders placed after 2 PM CAT will be processed the next business day.
  • You'll receive a confirmation email once your order ships.

2. Shipping Methods & Delivery Times

+
  • Standard Shipping (3-5 business days) - Free on orders over R500
  • Express Shipping (1-3 business days) - R99 flat rate
  • Same-Day Delivery (Lebowakgomo) - R150 (Order by 12 PM)

Note: Delivery times are estimates and may vary due to courier delays or public holidays.

3. Tracking Your Order

+
  • Once shipped, you'll receive a tracking number via email.
  • Track your package directly through our courier partners:
    • The Courier Guy
    • Aramex
    • PAXI
  • Contact us if you don't receive tracking within 48 hours of shipment.

4. Shipping Restrictions

+
  • We currently cannot ship to P.O. boxes.
  • Signature may be required for delivery of high-value orders.
  • Undeliverable packages will be returned to us, and a restocking fee may apply.
